Patrick Mason

Hey, my name is Patrick Mason. I am currently a Political Science Public Service major at UC Davis planning to go to Law School. I also was on the speech and debate team for 4 years and was captain and president. Speech and Debate will always have a huge place in my heart, as it helped me find a community. Through speech and debate I have made life long friendships and have a wealth of memories. Additionally it has given me tangible skills, including communication, negotiation, confidence, and resilience. During my time my favorite event was Parliamentary debate, where I was captain for 2 years. Joining speech made a tremendous difference in my life, and I'm so thankful for the program.

My name is Vassilios, and right now I'm a first-year at Caltech studying chemistry!
