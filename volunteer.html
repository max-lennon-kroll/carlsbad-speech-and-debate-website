Why Volunteer?

Our Purpose

The purpose of the CHS Speech and Debate Boosters is to promote the charitable and educational purposes of the Carlsbad High School Speech and Debate Team, as well as support the decisions of the coach.

Volunteer Opportunities

As they say, "it takes a village." And with a team of over 100 students competing, this is certainly true! Managing a team of this size is a huge undertaking, and we could not make it happen without our parent volunteers. We would love for all of our team members' parents to get involved with the booster club. If you cannot fill a leadership position, please join us at our booster club meetings and stay current on what's happening with our team!

We are required to provide judges for every tournament we attend. NO EXPERIENCE IS REQUIRED - we will train you! Most tournaments are on the weekends, making judging and chaperoning easier for working parents.

If you are interested in helping out, email us at Vpadmin@chssdboosters.org. We also welcome teams of two for any single role.

Other Ways To Support CHSSD

We always need and appreciate financial support to help offset tournament fees, NSDA membership fees, travel costs, technology costs, and more to help ensure that we are able to provide opportunities to ALL students. Please consider becoming a CHSSD Patron today. All donations are tax deductible.
